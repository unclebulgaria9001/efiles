## üîç What We Know (From Public Sources)

### Jeffrey Epstein - The Core Mystery

#### The Official Story
- **Career**: Teacher ‚Üí Bear Stearns ‚Üí Founded J. Epstein & Co. (1982)
- **Business**: Wealth management for billionaires ($1B minimum claimed)
- **Net Worth**: $500-600 million (source unclear)
- **Properties**: 6 major properties (Manhattan, Palm Beach, 2 islands, New Mexico, Paris)

#### The Problems with the Official Story
1. **Only ONE confirmed major client**: Leslie Wexner
2. **No documented investment track record**
3. **Opaque financial structure**
4. **Lifestyle exceeds explained income**
5. **No other billionaire clients identified**

### The Leslie Wexner Connection (CRITICAL)

**This is the key to understanding Epstein's wealth**

#### Who is Wexner?
- Founder of Limited Brands (Victoria's Secret, Bath & Body Works)
- Net worth: ~$7 billion
- One of America's richest people

#### The Relationship
- **Met**: Mid-1980s (circumstances unclear)
- **Power of Attorney**: Wexner gave Epstein complete financial control (1991)
- **Manhattan Mansion**: Transferred to Epstein (1995) - worth $77M
- **Duration**: ~20 years of close relationship
- **End**: Officially 2008 after Epstein's conviction

#### Unanswered Questions
1. How did they meet?
2. Why did Wexner trust Epstein so completely?
3. What services did Epstein actually provide?
4. How much money did Epstein manage?
5. Were there other clients like Wexner?
6. What really happened to the money?

**Wexner's Claims**:
- Cut ties after 2008
- Claims Epstein "misappropriated" funds
- Denies knowledge of trafficking
- Has NOT fully explained the relationship

### The Properties (The Empire)
